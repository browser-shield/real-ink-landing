FAQ

frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How accurate is the AI detection? +

Our detection uses multiple linguistic signals and achieves high accuracy on typical AI-generated content. However, no detector is 100% accurate - use it as one tool in your verification toolkit.

Does it work on all websites? +

Yes! Real Ink? works on any website with text content. You can also select specific text and analyze it via the context menu.

Is my data sent anywhere? +

Absolutely not. All processing happens locally in your browser. No text, no analytics, nothing leaves your device. Ever.

Will it slow down my browser? +

No. We've optimized for performance. The extension uses minimal CPU and memory, and you can configure it to scan only on demand.
