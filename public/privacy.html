Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Real Ink? does not collect any data. All processing happens locally on your device.

Data Collection

Real Ink? does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing information. The extension operates entirely offline and locally within your browser.

How It Works

When you use Real Ink?, the following happens:

  • Text analysis is performed locally in your browser using JavaScript
  • No text content is ever sent to any external server
  • No analytics or telemetry data is collected
  • No cookies or tracking mechanisms are used

Local Storage

The extension may use your browser's local storage to save your preferences (such as sensitivity settings). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared by removing the extension

Permissions

Real Ink? requires certain browser permissions to function:

  • Access to web pages: Required to analyze text content on pages you visit
  • Storage: Used to save your local preferences

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and never for data collection.

Third Parties

Real Ink? does not share any information with third parties because it does not collect any information in the first place.

Updates

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this policy. Any significant changes would require a new version of the extension.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our GitHub repository.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over the extension and can remove it at any time.
